About

Supporting Excellence
Through Safety Management

As a Terminal Operations Officer B assigned to the Safety Management Systems (SMS) Unit at the Mactan-Cebu International Airport Authority, I play a key role in fostering a proactive safety culture and ensuring the effective implementation of airport safety management practices. My responsibilities include supporting safety risk assessments, monitoring safety performance, investigating incidents and hazards, and promoting compliance with aviation safety regulations to maintain the highest standards of operational safety.

Working within one of the Philippines' busiest international gateways, I contribute to the continuous improvement of safety processes that protect passengers, employees, stakeholders, and airport assets. Through data-driven safety initiatives and collaboration with various airport departments, I help ensure that airport operations remain safe, efficient, and aligned with both national and international aviation standards.
